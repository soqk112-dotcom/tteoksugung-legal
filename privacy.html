Privacy Policy — tteoksugung

Last updated: 2026-07-15

What data we access

tteoksugung connects to a single TikTok account (the owner's own account) via TikTok's Content Posting API, solely to publish video content on that account's behalf.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect data from any TikTok user other than the account owner.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • We do not use TikTok data for advertising or analytics purposes.

Data retention

Video files and generated captions used for posting are processed transiently through an automation pipeline (Make.com) and are not retained beyond what's needed to complete the posting workflow. Access tokens are stored securely within the automation platform's credential vault and are not exposed to third parties.
